AulaNauts

The term 'AulaNaut' has been specially coined for Team AulaCube; 'Aula' stands for 'classroom' in Latin, signifying our focus on education, while 'Naut' symbolizes exploration, representing our collective journey towards innovation in the field of education.

At AulaCube, we pride ourselves on having a team of highly talented and mission-focused individuals. Our AulaNauts are distinguished alumni from prestigious institutes such as IIM Kozhikode, NIT Silchar, NSUT Delhi, TISS, IIT Bombay, and VIT Vellore. This diverse educational background is complemented by their rich professional experiences in leading companies like Flipkart, Samsung, Pharmeasy, and Atlassian.

Each AulaNaut brings a unique perspective and a wealth of knowledge, contributing to our goal of redefining educational experiences through innovative technology. Together, we're committed to creating transformative solutions that resonate with our vision of making education more accessible, engaging, and effective.
