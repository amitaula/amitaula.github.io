What is AulaCube

We are AulaCube, a technology-driven company passionate about revolutionizing education. Our name AulaCube symbolizes the classroom ('Aula') as a cube of innovation, knowledge, and community. Our solutions leverage AI to offer a unique educational experience.

Our Products

AulaNest: For Pre-schools

AulaNest focuses on enriching the preschool experience by bridging the gap between parents and schools, providing timely updates, progress reports, and fostering better communication.
